What Can Be Reported

This section is intended for reporting any incidents that directly impact or hinder sales activities. These may include, but are not limited to:

  • Network or service outages affecting customer experience or new activations
  • System or portal issues (e.g. CRM errors, login problems)
  • SIM/MBB Device stock shortages or delays in inventory replenishment
  • Customer complaints that are recurring or unresolved and affecting sales
  • Weather Impact or similar issues directly effecting sales

Please provide as much detail as possible when submitting an incident to ensure timely resolution.
